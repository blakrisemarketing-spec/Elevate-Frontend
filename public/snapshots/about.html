Rosemary Agyeiwah Great-Damzi

Co-founder

Rosemary joined Elevate Career Hub as a co-founder in 2021. When she started her first job in 2018, after completing her bachelor’s degree in accounting, she realized that she was ill-prepared for the job market despite graduating with 1<sup>st</sup> Class Honors. After learning a few lessons the hard way and providing direct mentorship to a few people, she decided to use LinkedIn, through her ‘Dear Young Professional’ series to reach out to more young people and educate them on things she wished she had known before she started working. The great feedback she received from that initiative led her to start a mentoring community named ‘Mentoring With Rosemary’ which impacted the lives of many young professionals.

In 2021, she received the offer to join Elevate Career Hub where she continues to leverage her expertise and passion for mentorship to empower individuals to achieve their career aspirations. Her innovative approach to career development and her unwavering dedication to supporting others make her a valuable asset to the organization and a driving force behind its mission to empower the next generation of professionals. 

As the sole recipient of the fully-funded GDI Merit Scholarship at the University of Manchester in 2022, she is instrumental in assisting our grad school clients to secure substantive scholarships and admissions into top universities all over the world. 

Rosemary is also a member of the Association of Chartered Certified Accountants (United Kingdom).

General Questions

What Services do you offer?

Elevate Career Hub offers a range of career and educational services, including resume writing, interview coaching, school selection guidance, reference letter drafting, and more.

Getting started is easy! Simply explore our website to learn more about our services, and then schedule a consultation with one of our experts to discuss your specific needs and goals.

Yes, our services are highly customizable and can be tailored to meet the unique needs of clients across various industries and fields.
