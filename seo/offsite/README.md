# Off-Site Authority Queue

On-page content gets us ranked; off-site signals (links, mentions, citations) get us
trusted. This folder is the human-action queue the weekly routine tops up. The agent
does NOT send outreach or submit listings on its own; it researches concrete, live
targets and angles, and a human executes them.

- `directory-targets.md` — relevant directories, listings, and profiles to claim or
  submit to (career services, education consultancies, Ghana business directories,
  study-abroad portals).
- `digital-pr-angles.md` — story angles a journalist, newsletter, or partner would
  actually want, tied to the founders' real experience and Elevate's data.

## Rules

- **Live, specific targets only.** A directory entry needs a real URL and the actual
  submission path, not "submit to career directories".
- **No fabricated credentials or stats** in any outreach draft. Use the canonical
  facts (6+ years, 96%, 2,000+) and real founder stories only.
- **The agent drafts and queues; a human approves and sends.** Outreach is an
  outward-facing action and stays human-gated.
