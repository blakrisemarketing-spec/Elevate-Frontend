# Reports

Dated analytics, rank, and GEO-citation reports written by the weekly routine
(`seo-weekly`) and the occasional baseline/keyword-research pass.

## Naming

- `weekly-YYYY-Www.md` — the weekly analytics + re-prioritization report.
- `baseline-YYYY-MM.md` — a point-in-time snapshot (e.g. when GSC is first connected).
- `keyword-research-YYYY-MM.md` — a focused keyword-volume pull.

## What the weekly report contains

- Top GSC queries + pages (clicks, impressions, CTR, position).
- Current Ghana SERP position for each published article's primary keyword (DataForSEO).
- Week-over-week movement vs the previous report.
- The GEO citation scoreboard: for 3 to 5 priority queries, is `elevatecareerhub.com`
  present in Google results / AI Overviews, and do ChatGPT / Perplexity / Copilot
  cite it? A simple present / not-yet per query, tracked over time.
- Quick wins (page-2 queries, low-CTR queries) and gaps (high-impression queries we
  do not yet target).
- The roadmap changes made this week.

No report exists yet. The first one lands after credentials are configured and
`seo-weekly` runs. Until then, all roadmap `volume` fields stay `null`.
